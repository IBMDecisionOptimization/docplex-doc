IBM Decision Optimization CPLEX Modeling for Python - Technical Preview
=======================================================================

Welcome to IBM Decision Optimization CPLEX Modeling for Python - Technical Preview.

With this library, you can quickly and easily add the power of optimization to your 
application. You can model your problems by using the Python API and solve 
them on the cloud with the IBM Decision Optimization on Cloud service or on your 
computer with IBM ILOG CPLEX Optimization Studio.

You can read more about IBM Decision Optimization on Cloud
`here <https://onboarding-oaas.docloud.ibmcloud.com/software/analytics/docloud>`_ and
its free trial version `here <https://developer.ibm.com/docloud/try-docloud-free>`_.


You can read more about IBM ILOG CPLEX Optimization Studio 
`here <http://www-03.ibm.com/software/products/category/prescriptive-analytics>`_
and its free Community Edition `here <http://www-01.ibm.com/software/websphere/products/optimization/cplex-studio-community-edition>`_.
